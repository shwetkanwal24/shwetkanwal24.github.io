Coronavirus: Spain sees lowest daily toll in 17 days, with 605 deaths

Spain recorded its lowest daily death toll from coronavirus in 17 days, with 605 people dying, the government said on Friday.The update raised the overall number of fatalities to 15,843 in Spain, which has suffered one of the worst outbreaks of COVID-19 in the world, and where the total number of cases now stands at 157,022.The novel cononarius, which originated from China's Wuhan, has infected over 1 million people and has over 80,000 dead globally.
